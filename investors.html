The Pitch: BLD Santa Monica

The restaurant industry is suffocating under the weight of its own complexity. Endless menus, substitution requests, and bloated labor costs kill margins. BLD Santa Monica is the antidote. We are bringing a radical return to simplicity to the iconic intersection of Santa Monica Boulevard and Ocean Avenue.

The Concept: Engineered for Efficiency

We offer three meals, three prices, and zero decisions. Our minimalist menu provides exactly one expertly crafted option for Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner. Every price includes tax, and our no-tipping model ensures equitable pay while eliminating friction. We use French culinary techniques to elevate American comfort food, achieving fine-dining quality with casual dining speed.

The Economics: Lean and Highly Profitable

Simplicity is a massive financial advantage. By eliminating menu bloat, we drastically reduce our Cost of Goods Sold and cross-utilize ingredients across dayparts.

  • The Raise: We are seeking $2,000,000 to fully capitalize buildout, launch, and six months of working capital.
  • The Model: Our blended food cost sits at an incredibly low 27.3%.
  • The Trajectory: We project hitting break-even by Month 16.
  • The Return: Year 3 projects $2,628,000 in revenue with a 14.6% EBITDA margin ($385,000). Early projections estimate a 3.7x ROI within the first five years.

The Edge: Built for the Modern Consumer

This is a highly Instagramable, aesthetically driven space designed to attract tourists, locals, and digital influencers. We operate with a lean staff, utilizing advanced kitchen technology and cashless POS systems to keep overhead low and throughput rapid.
