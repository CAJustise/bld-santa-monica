Market Opportunity

Santa Monica has consistent daytime density and high discretionary spend. BLD captures breakfast commuters, lunch professionals, and dinner leisure demand under one operational model.

The concept leverages a compact menu architecture with prep overlap to drive speed of service, labor efficiency, and a higher mix of high-margin beverages.

  • Foot traffic corridors within 8 minutes of beach and office clusters
  • Daypart diversification reduces volatility versus single-occasion concepts
  • Brandable format for Westside replication after flagship traction
